Welcome to X_Weapons, a chat command script for all of your loadouts!
____________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Inside this package you will find all the necessary files needed for this!
The "main.lua" file is where everything will be!

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________

If you have some basic "LUA" knowledge you can easily change some of the loadouts of this script or even 
add more loadouts to the script!

____________________________________________________________________________________________________________

Functionality:
This script listens for chat commands and then give you your loadouts
____________________________________________________________________________________________________________
Commands:

/locop | Gives you the basic Police Dept. loadout:
Combat Pistol
Carbine Rifle
Flare
Pump Shotgun
Taser
Baton
Flashlight
____________________

/loswat | Gives you a SWAT loadout:
Combat Pistol
Carbine Rifle
Special Carbine
Pump Shotgun
Assault Shotgun
Flare
Taser
Baton
Flashlight
____________________

/loems | Gives you a basic EMS loadout:
Taser
Flashlight 
Switchblade
____________________

/lofire | Gives you a basic Fire Dept. loadout:
Fire Extinguisher
Hatchet
Flashlight
Flare
Crowbar
____________________

/loremove | Removes all weapons in your inventory:
Like said above, just removes weapons in your inventory
